# Greeting

Compose a friendly greeting that fits the context.

## Instructions

1. Identify the recipient, the occasion, and the desired tone.
2. Choose a salutation that matches the formality:
   - Formal: `Dear <name>,`
   - Neutral: `Hi <name>,`
   - Casual: `Hey <name>!`
3. Add one short, warm sentence relevant to the occasion.
4. Output only the greeting — no commentary.

## Example

Input: welcome a new teammate named Sam to the design team.

Output:

> Hi Sam, welcome to the design team — we're really glad to have you on board!
